Lemon Pepper Wings

Crispy baked lemon pepper wings basted with lemon pepper seasoning and melted butter.
Toss in oil and bake for an hour at 400F. Finished off with lemon pepper and butter. Enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 4 lbs chicken wings
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 2 tbsp lemon-pepper seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400F
  2. Toss wings in oil
  3. Place wings on wire rack set on a baking sheet, try not to crowd the wings
  4. Bake for 50-60 minutes or until golden brown and crispy
  5. Remove wings from oven
  6. Mix melted butter and lemon-pepper seasoning
  7. Brush seasoning liberally onto wings
  8. Place wings on platter and enjoy!
