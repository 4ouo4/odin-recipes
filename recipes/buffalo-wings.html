Buffalo Wings

Your classic American saucy red wings.
Chuck it in the oven. Toss it in the sauce. Enjoy that shit.

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs chicken wings
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp garlic powder

Buffalo Sauce Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup Franks Original Red Hot Sauce
  • 1 tbsp granulated sugar, or brown sugar

Steps

  1. Pat dry chicken with paper towel
  2. Preheat oven to 450F
  3. Line rimmed baking sheet with foil and place wire rack over the pan
  4. Combine baking powder, salt, and garlic powder in a bowl. Sprinkle over chicken and toss to combine.
  5. Arrange chicken on prepared wire rack
  6. Bake chicken for 25 minutes, flip over and bake for another 25 minutes or until crisp and cooked through
  7. Combine sauce ingredients in a bowl
  8. Toss chicken in sauce and enjoy
